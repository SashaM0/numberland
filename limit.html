DOWN TO THE RABBIT-HOLE, DOWN TO

the Limit of a Function

Alice philosophically declares that in the end, she might disappear altogether, "still go down like a candle"; this reasoning may reflect the concept of the function limit.

The limit of a function is like figuring out where the function is heading as you get extremely close, almost like zooming in on a map to see where you're going. It helps us understand what value the function is getting close to or approaching.
